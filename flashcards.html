Series 65 Mastery Flashcards — Print Instructions

This deck is laid out for Avery 5388 (3″ × 5″ perforated index cards, 3 per Letter sheet), but works equally well on plain cardstock with manual cutting.

How to Print

  1. Open this file in Chrome or Edge. Press Ctrl + P.
  2. Destination: your printer.
  3. Paper size: Letter. Orientation: Portrait.
  4. Margins: None (or Default; the layout has its own margins).
  5. Scale: 100% (do not "Fit to page").
  6. Two-sided / Duplex: ON — Flip on Long Edge. This is critical so card backs land behind their fronts.
  7. Background graphics: ON (under "More settings") so the faint cut lines print.
  8. Print one test sheet first. Hold to a light — the back of card #1 should sit directly behind the front of card #1.

Cardstock

  • Avery 5388 perforated cards: load, print, snap apart. Ignore the dashed border — the perforations cut cleanly.
  • Plain 65–110 lb cardstock: print, then cut along the dashed borders with a paper trimmer.

Deck Organization

Cards are grouped by section in the order you'll study them. The card ID at the bottom right shows section and number (e.g. LAW-042). The little F / B in the bottom-left is just a sanity check that front and back are aligned.
